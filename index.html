Margherita Bonan

Hi! I am Margherita, currently a third-year bachelor student in Applied Mathematics at the University of Groningen. In this page you can find information about my ongoing projects and experiences, as well as my contact details.

Projects

  • Dec 2024 - Present: iGEM 2025 Competition Team Member

    As part of the University of Groningen's team for the iGEM 2025 competition, I contributed to the dry lab section by applying machine learning models to predict optimal guide RNAs for detecting antibiotic resistance traits using CRISPR-Cas13, integral to developing a novel diagnostic kit, "CRIKIT". We are furthermore working on developing a software that automizes the process of analysis of the fluorescence data to detect antibiotic resistance sequences.

    You can follow the most recent updates of this project on LinkedIn and Instagram.

    Learn more about the competition on the iGEM Official Website.
